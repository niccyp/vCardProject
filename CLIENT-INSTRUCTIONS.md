## üé® Photo Guidelines

### Profile Photo
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: Minimum 400x400px
- **Aspect Ratio**: Square (1:1)
- **Quality**: High resolution, well-lit
- **Background**: Clean, professional
- **Face**: Clearly visible, centered

### Cover Photo
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 1200x400px or similar aspect ratio (3:1)
- **Content**: Company logo, branded background, or professional image
- **Quality**: High resolution
- **Note**: A color gradient will be used if no cover photo is uploaded

## ‚ùì FAQ

**Q: What if I don't have a cover photo?**  
A: No problem! A color gradient matching your theme will be used instead.

**Q: Can I change my information later?**  
A: Yes! You can update your vCard anytime for free. Just generate a new one and send it to us.

**Q: What image formats are supported?**  
A: JPG, PNG, and most common image formats work fine.

**Q: My profile photo looks stretched - what should I do?**  
A: Use a square image (same width and height). The system will automatically crop it to a circle.

**Q: Can I add more social media links?**  
A: Currently, the form supports Website and LinkedIn. For additional links, please contact us directly.

**Q: How long does it take to get my cards?**  
A: Standard production time is 3-5 business days for in-stock colors, 5-7 days for custom colors.
